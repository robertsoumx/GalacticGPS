Issue: UniverseGenerator - insertion sorting; the neighbor searching is inaccurate
AI tool: Deepseek
Prompt: My implementation of a linked list is not working, what might be the issue?
Solution found: The AI pointed out I am comparing my nodes in a 1D manner when it should be 2D. This
                relates to other issues as well, duplication of my nodes, unbalanced tree, and inaccuracy
                in neighbor searching --> adding a depth parameter fixes it. Alternating the comparison
                in X and Y yields an accurate 2D representation. I.e Earth = (100,300), Mars = (101,3000)
                My old algorithm would think it is close because it only compares the X value when in
                reality it is very far apart because of the difference in its Y value.
